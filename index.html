Motorsport Immersion
Core Program · Since 2017

Veterans and medically releasing service members are embedded within professional racing teams across North America. No prior motorsport experience required — only a willingness to participate.

Participants take on crew support, creative, or series-level roles — attending the racetrack approximately once monthly for five-day activations, for up to two racing seasons.

"It brought back a piece of me that was lost when I left the Army."

Diversionary Therapy
eMotorsports · Car Corrals

Recreational motorsports used as a therapeutic tool — a "distractor task" that complements clinical treatment and fosters personal growth, self-esteem, and community.

Car Corrals — track-side experiences at racing events including parade laps and connections with local car clubs for ongoing activities like driver education, car shows, and rally drives.

eMotorsports — a virtual iRacing League where beneficiaries, veterans, and supporters compete together. Operation Motorsport covers the initial three-month subscription for qualified participants. All events are livestreamed on YouTube.

Veterans Race of Remembrance
Flagship Event · VIR · Nov 2026

North America's premier endurance motorsport event honoring military service — raising funds for Operation Motorsport's mission while celebrating and remembering those who served.

The 2026 event at VIRginia International Raceway features an 8-hour multi-class endurance race with a 30-minute Remembrance Service pause, plus a SuperGP Arrive & Drive Championship.
